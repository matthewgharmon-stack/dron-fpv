FAQ

What types of properties do you film?

Offices, apartments, single-family and multifamily homes, retail, industrial, hospitality, and new developments. We tailor the shot list to the property’s use and follow all building rules and access requirements.

How far in advance should I book?

Ideally 5–7 days. For larger portfolios or multi-city travel, 7–10 days is best. Rush slots may be available—tell us your dates in

How should I prepare the property?

Tidy and declutter, turn on all lights, align blinds/curtains, hide personal items, secure pets, and confirm access/parking. We’ll share a quick prep checklist when you schedule.

How long does a typical session take?

Usually 2–3 hours depending on square footage, access, and add-ons (photos, reels, 3D). We coordinate an efficient plan to minimize disruption..
